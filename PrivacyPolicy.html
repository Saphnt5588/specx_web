SPECX Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

SPECX ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by SPECX when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Website, including:

  • **Personal Identification Data:** Name, email address, phone number, and postal address when you register for an account or make a purchase.
  • **Technical Data:** Internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting, operating system, and platform.
  • **Usage Data:** Information about how you use our website, products viewed or searched for, page response times, download errors, and page interaction information (such as scrolling, clicks, and mouse-overs).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our Website and services.
  • To process transactions and manage your orders, returns, and exchanges.
  • To improve our Website, products, and user experience.
  • To communicate with you, including sending technical notices, updates, and marketing and promotional messages (where permitted).
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • **Service Providers:** Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing and data analysis.
  • **Legal Compliance:** If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
  • **Business Transfers:** In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company.

4. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • **Access:** The right to request copies of your personal data.
  • **Correction:** The right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • **Erasure:** The right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • **Objection to Processing:** The right to object to our processing of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
